How It Works



There are two claws in SIAM that pick up each sock and sorts them by color and size. They are made of recycled material so they would be cheaper, but still as sturdy. The claws work together to sort the socks, putting them into different piles according to their size and color.


This is the de-wrinkle spray dispenser. The user pours the liquid spray into this. The dispenser then sprays it onto the shirt in order to remove most of the wrinkles from it.
